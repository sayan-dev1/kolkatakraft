    Privacy Policy

    Last updated: April 2024

    🍪 Cookie Usage

    KolkataKraft uses session cookies to maintain your login state. These cookies are:

    • Session-based (cleared when browser closes)
    • Used only for authentication and basic analytics
    • Never shared with third-party advertisers
    • Encrypted server-side

    📊 Analytics Data

    We collect anonymous page view data (pages visited, products viewed) to improve user experience. This data is stored in our own database and never shared externally. You can opt out by using our site in incognito mode.

    🔐 Data Security

    Passwords are hashed using bcrypt (industry standard). All sessions use secure HTTP-only cookies. In a production environment, all traffic would be served over HTTPS (TLS 1.3).

    Legal & Compliance

    KolkataKraft Platform Policies

    📋 GST Awareness

    KolkataKraft is designed to be GST-compliant. For B2C transactions, applicable GST rates (typically 5% or 12% for handicrafts) are included in the displayed price. GST invoices are generated for all completed orders. Artisans with turnover above ₹20 lakhs are required to register for GST.

    ⚠️ Demo Notice: This is an MVP/demo platform. GST computations shown are for illustrative purposes. For production deployment, integrate with a certified GST API provider.

    🛡️ Platform Terms

    Artisans are responsible for accurate product descriptions and legal compliance. KolkataKraft acts as a marketplace facilitator and does not hold inventory. Dispute resolution is handled through our admin panel within 14 working days.

    ♻️ Ethical Sourcing

    We are committed to fair wages for artisans, sustainable craft practices, and preserving traditional techniques. No platform commissions are charged on artisan earnings above ₹10,000/month.
